About Furina Companion

Furina Companion is a comprehensive mental health support platform designed to provide emotional assistance, mindfulness exercises, and access to professional counselling resources. Our AI-powered companion is here for you 24/7, offering a safe space to express yourself without judgment.

Our mission is to make mental health support accessible to everyone, bridging the gap between self-help and professional care through technology that understands and responds to your unique needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure and private? +

Yes, absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All conversations with Furina are encrypted and we never share your personal data with third parties without consent. You can request data deletion at any time.

How does the counselling connection work? +

Our platform connects you with licensed therapists based on your needs and preferences. After completing the assessment, you'll receive recommendations for therapists who specialize in your areas of concern.

Is Furina a replacement for professional therapy? +

No, Furina is designed to be a supportive companion and resource, but not a replacement for professional mental health care. For serious concerns, we always recommend consulting with a licensed healthcare provider.

How accurate is the mental health assessment? +

Our assessment is based on clinically validated screening tools, but it's meant to be informative rather than diagnostic. The results can help you understand potential areas of concern and guide you toward appropriate resources.
